POISSON DISTRIBUTION

Y ~ Poisson(Lambda)
n is very large, say 10000
p is very small, say 0.00024
Mean = np
(Here, =2.4)
Variance = np(1-p)
	~= Mean (Since 1-p -> 1)
So, Lambda = Mean = Variance
Lambda is the only parameter.
Unlike Binomial, where we had n and p.

P(k) = (e^-lambda.lambda^k)/k!
